Wireframe

This color palette was chosen from the different colors I've been playing around with along these projects. It feels vintage-inspired, but still fresh and trendy. There is a highlight color, a dark color for shadows/legibility, a bolder warm tone, a bolder cool tone, and a mid-tone to round it out. The gradient was introduced as a way to help push the design further and encourage a dream-like aesthetic. There is purple and orange text laid over each other to create a kind of 3D effect. There is a bold san-serif font for display, and an easily distinguishable serif font for body text.
